A little about me

I graduated from the University of Vermont with a degree in Biology and a minor in Chemistry. I took my first job as a Pharmacy Technician at Edge Pharmacy Services in Vermont. I developed many skills including a keen attention to detail, product and process development and implementation, QA/QC best practices, working on multiple projects at one time, and time management. I rose to a leadership role in the company as the Non-Sterile department head, managing the production of specialized medications.

My girlfriend and I decided it was time to leave Vermont and head out west to Portland, OR. I am very interested in renewable energy technologies and took a job as a Solar Installer at Imagine Energy. Solar installation is a very team oriented work environment and I gained many valuable skills such as effective communication, communication under pressure, and collaborative problem solving. Through my work, I learned a lot about the industry and was exposed to technologies used in energy production. I became very interested in those, and other technologies and realized I wanted a career in the technology industry.

I found that The Tech Academy would be my best opportunity to put myself in a position to impact the world through technology. I recently completed The Tech Academys Software Development program, taking courses in Version Control, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, Visual Studio, Python, C#, and .NET Framework, among other material. I am dedicated and hard working and I am very excited to start out in my career as a Software Developer. I am eager to learn new skills and contribute effectively to any project I am a part of.
